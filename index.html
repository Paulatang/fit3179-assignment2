About Starbucks

Starbucks established its brand in 51 years ago where it opened its first store along the cobblestone streets of Seattle’s historic Pike Place Market.

The variety of products offered for its customers to take home include fresh-roasted coffee beans, tea, and spices from around the world.

Since then, Starbucks expanded to other countries in 34,317 locations, and built a strong brand reputation worldwide.

Customer perception towards Starbucks

In general, the majority of customers are satisfied with Starbucks’ products, promotions, ambiences, and services and choose options.

However, Starbucks may need to improve its prices and wifi to retain and gain more purchasers. For example, Starbucks could upgrade its wifi service by finding a better internet service provider.

Promotions received by customers

Social media is the most popular and common platform where Starbucks promotes its brand successfully, while the in-store display is the least effective promotional method.

Starbucks could therefore put more resources into social media and improve its in-store display to attract more new customers.
